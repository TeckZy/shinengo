    About

    Who we are

    Non-Governmental Organization

    Education is fundamental to human progress. It plays a prominent role in all-around development of individual as well as society. Without education we are incomplete.

    What We Do:

    Every child must go to school in his/her appropriate age as everyone has equal rights for education from birth. We, SHINE Education and Welfare Society, provide financial, moral support to poor children and encourage them to gain knowledge by handling over textbooks, stationaries, magzines, refreshments to attract them, etc.

    Aim

    What We Think

    Our Mission

    Our prime focus is on providing proactive education at minimal cost. We focus on rural areas mostly but will expand for low-income urban families for sure. Also, we have versatile objectives of providing incentives during calamities like extreme winter, flood etc. We also spread awareness about health as and when necessary.

    Our Plan

    We focus on fruitful and regular team meetings. Here we modify our agendas and add some more strategies as the time comes. Since there is acute shortage of funds with changing times, we abide by maintaining emergency fund and use it wisely. We promote through social media as to reach maximum adults, teens, and seniors.

    Our Vision

    Our one and only prime target is to eliminating absolute illiteracy. Thus, we choose rural areas to start with. Our secondary goals remain providing relief according to our capacity and gathering government help through our reach. We believe in building an exchange tunnel from the donors to the beneficiaries, so no abundance gives just an ordinary return.

    Work

    What We Do

    EDUCATION HUT SCHOOL

    Education hut is a place where bare necessities are available for education. At education Huts, we run school (class 1-3) and teach tuition for classes beyond it. For this we hire teachers and provide basic facilities at zero cost. There are no tuition fees, and the teachers are paid for their services by our NGO. In this we are also helped by parents who adopt a kid from Education Huts. Such parents pay for their kid's education fees (₹1500/ year/ kid), thus reducing our financial burden and maintaining quality education.

    Read More

    HEALTH CAN'T WAIT

    Health being the most neglected sector of the society, is becoming the need of the hour. We try to spread awareness about it with the help of medical camps, sanitary pad distribution, dental checkups etc. This is to bring an inclusive nature for the healthcare, so it accommodates more people. We also collect funds for the medical treatment requests from our area within our capacity. Till now we have successfully helped 2 hand plasters of widow women, 1 backbone injury and 1 Pelvic bone surgery.

    Read More

    DISASTER RESPONSE TEAM

    Our base being in Gorakhpur (East Uttar Pradesh) falls in Zone 4 of the earthquake scale. Also, it is a Tarai region hence has seen frequent floods. Such calamities really need a large working force and hence we equip ourselves to provide our volunteers as and when the need arises. We also collect funds for the medical treatment requests from our area within our capacity. Till now we have successfully helped 2 hand plasters of widow women, 1 backbone injury and 1 Pelvis bone surgery.
